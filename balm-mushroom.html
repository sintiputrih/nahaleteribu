Pokémon Global Link artwork
Introduced in Generation V

The Balm Mushroom (Japanese: かおるキノコ Fragrant Mushroom), formatted BalmMushroom prior to Generation VI, is a valuable item introduced in Generation V.

In the core series games

Price

In the Generation V games, the BalmMushroom can be sold to the gourmet maniac on Route 5 for $25000.

In Pokémon Sword and Shield, the Balm Mushroom can be sold to the valuable item buyer in Stow-on-Side for $25,000.

Effect

The Balm Mushroom serves no practical purpose other than to be sold.

In the spin-off games

Pokémon Quest

A Balm Mushroom in Pokémon Quest

A Balm Mushroom is an ingredient that can be used in cooking to attract wild Pokémon in Pokémon Quest. It is categorized as a Mushroom.

Description

GamesDescription
QuestSoft and precious ingredients.

Acquisition

Balm Mushroom is a possible ingredient that can be obtained by completing expeditions. Balm Mushrooms are most commonly found in the areas Parched Peak and Farside Fjord. They can also be obtained randomly by recycling Power Stones.
