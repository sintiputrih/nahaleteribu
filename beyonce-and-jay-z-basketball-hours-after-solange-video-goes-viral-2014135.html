Beyonce, Jay Z Smile At Basketball Game After Solange Fight Video Leak

Publish date: 2024-09-24

It was business as usual for Beyonce and Jay Z last night, as the duo hit the basketball court, refusing to keep a low profile after video footage of Solange Knowles' elevator attack went viral earlier that day.

The musical power couple were once again courtside at Brooklyn's Barclays Center on Monday evening, May 12, looking happy and relaxed as they chatted together while watching the Brooklyn Nets play Miami Heat.

Queen Bey appeared unfazed by the release of the shocking security video showing her sister Solange, 27, launching a violent attack against her husband Jay Z in an elevator of The Standard hotel after the Met Gala on May 5.

The 32-year-old looked stunning as she laughed and relaxed in her courtside seat, wearing a blue denim jumpsuit and black high heels. Her rapper husband Jay Z, 44, wore a white T-shirt and black jeans with a heavy gold chain around his neck.
