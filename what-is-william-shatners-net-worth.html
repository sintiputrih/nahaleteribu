What is William Shatner's net worth?

Publish date: 2024-02-02

STAR Trek icon William Shatner's life has been full of exciting adventures - from his acting career to being the world's oldest person to go to space.

But what is the actor's net worth? Here's everything you need to know.

What is William Shatner's net worth?

William Shatner was born on March 22, 1931, in Montreal, Canada.

He is not only an actor but also an author, producer, director, screenwriter and singer.

His estimated net worth is $100million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Most of his income comes from playing Captain James T. Kirk in the sci-fi television series Star Trek.

He also had lead roles in TJ Hooker, The Practice and Boston Legal.

In 2000, he co-starred in the movie Miss Congeniality and the sequel, Miss Congeniality 2: Armed And Fabulous, in 2004.

Shatner made headlines again on October 12, 2021, as he travelled into space on Jeff Bezos’ Blue Origin mission.

His 11-minute journey marked him as the oldest person to travel to outer space in history.

Is William Shatner married?

Shatner has been married four times.

In August 1956, he married Canadian actress Gloria Rand and they had three daughters, Leslie, Lisabeth and Melanie.

They got divorced in 1969.

From 1973-1996, he was married to Marcy Lafferty.

He married Nerine Kidd Shatner in 1997.

In 1999, Nerine was found at the bottom of their swimming pool.
