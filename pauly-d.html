Pauly D Latest News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-03-10

DJ and reality star Pauly D is best known for being a cast member of the MTV series Jersey Shore. Pauly D was part of the original cast of the show and has gone on to star in the spinoffs The Pauly D Project and Jersey Shore: Family Vacation. He has also appeared on the dating shows Famously Single, Game of Clones, and Double Shot at Love.

Along with reality TV, Pauly D has an impressive music and DJing career, releasing his first single, "Beat Dat Beat (It's Time To)" in 2010, and his debut album "Back to Love" in 2012. He has also had DJ residencies in Las Vegas and Atlantic City.

In 2013, Pauly D welcomed his daughter Amabella Sophia with his ex-girlfriend, Amanda Markert. He is currently dating Nikki Hall, who he met on Double Shot at Love in 2020. In 2020, he also appeared on the MTV show, Revenge Prank, with his BFF Vinny Guadagnino.
