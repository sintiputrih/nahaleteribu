Eat Your Vegetables TikTok Song Lyrics &amp; Vegan Teacher Trend Videos Explored

Publish date: 2024-06-13

You must be living under a rock if you haven’t heard about the Vegan Teacher. Who is she, and Where did the Eat Your Vegetables TikTok come from? Also, learn about the song’s lyrics and trend videos.

That Vegan Teacher’s ‘Eat Your Vegetables’ is a new viral TikTok song that has taken the internet by storm. That Vegan Teacher, aka Miss Kadie, is a Youtuber and Tiktoker.

She is widely known for calling out other celebrities to go vegan. Gordon Ramsay and YouTuber Pewdiepie are some of the famous people that she has called out.

Her real name is Kadie Karen Diekmeyer, and she is originally from Canada. Kadie is an animal rights activist and promotes veganism through her videos.

One of her videos got made into a song by netizens. The song has blown up on Tiktok, and people have posted various funny, dance, and transition videos to it.

Eat Your Vegetables TikTok Song Lyrics

Eat Your Vegetable is a fanmade song that mashes the audio from one of That Vegan Teacher’s videos and the song ‘And To Those I Love, Thanks For Sticking Around’ by $UICIDEBOY$.

The Tiktoker @sulfateoctagon created the original remix, and it has 7.8 million views on the platform.

The song starts with That vegan Teacher saying the line “Eat your vegetables.” At the end of the sentence, a boosted autotune sound effect is added to her voice, and the song smoothly transitions to ‘And To Those I Love, Thanks For Sticking Around.’

The Tiktok ends with the song’s lyrics, “one last pic, and I’ll be gone.”
