Chas McCormick Parents & Family Life

His MLB Journey

Chas attended Henderson High School and helped its baseball team to Ches-Mont titles in 2010 and 2013. After graduating, he went to the Millersville University of Pennsylvania to play college baseball.

McCormick played for the Millersville Marauders for four seasons. Afterward, the Houston Astros drafted him in the 21st round of the 2017 MLB draft.

Chase spent the 2017 season with the Buies Creek Astros, Quad Cities River Bandits, and Greeneville Astros. Moreover, he played with Corpus Christi Hooks and Buies Creek Astros in 2018.

In 2019, he played with the Round Rock Express and Corpus Christi Hooks. Furthermore, he spent his time in the Dominican Republic, playing for the Leones del Escogido.


On 1st April 2021, he made his MLB debut against the Oakland Athletics. According to his 2021 starts, he batted .257/.319/.447 with 14 home runs and 50 RBIs in 284 at-bats.
