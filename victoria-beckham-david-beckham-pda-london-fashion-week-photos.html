David Beckham Kissed Victoria Beckham at London Fashion Week, &amp; I'm Dead

Publish date: 2024-06-05
  • Victoria and David Beckham further quashed divorce rumors when they attended the Kent & Curwen presentation during London Fashion Week Men's this weekend.
  • New photos of the couple show them engaging in PDA, and laughing with one another as they enjoy the event. Needless to say, they look totally loved-up and adorable.
  • The couple's eldest son, Brooklyn Beckham, was also in attendance, along with his new girlfriend, Hana Cross.

Victoria and David Beckham are laying divorce rumors to rest. Sitting side-by-side at London Fashion Week Men's, the Beckhams proved that they're still very much together, thank you very much.

Together, David and Victoria have four children—sons Brooklyn, Romeo, and Cruz, and daughter Harper. Back in September 2018, Victoria Beckham celebrated the 10th anniversary of her eponymous brand at London Fashion Week, and she was supported by her husband and their four children, all of whom were extremely proud of the designer. Posting on Instagram about the show, son Romeo wrote, "Amazing show mum love you so much @victoriabeckham ❤️."

Back in June 2018, a representative for Victoria Beckham released a statement regarding divorce rumors to Harper's BAZAAR UK saying, "This is nonsense." And judging by their latest joint appearance, the Beckhams are stronger than ever.
