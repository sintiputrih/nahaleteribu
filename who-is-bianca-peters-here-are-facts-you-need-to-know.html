Who Is Bianca Peters?

Bianca Castillo Peters (a.k.a Bianca Peters) was born in Miami, Florida, on April 1, 1991, into a family of footballers and sports enthusiasts. Therefore, it came as no surprise when little Bianca aspired to pursue a career in professional football. One of her uncles was a footballer and as of this writing, she has a cousin in active football. Although not much is available as to the names of her parents and siblings if any, we do know that Bianca Peters’ family was a loud one when it came to sports, but that didn’t affect her educational pursuit.

She attended the California State University, Long Beach, where she graduated “summa cum laude” with a bachelor’s degree in communications. She soon moved to Minnesota to pursue a career in sports. But as life continued to have its way, Peters rather saw herself “talking” about sports rather than playing it.

Career

Earlier on in college, Bianca Peters worked for the student-run TV station, College Beat, where she produced and hosted several events including a college football show, which aired weekly. From there it was a long ride across several other TV stations and sports outlets. She moved to ESPN where she covered X-Games. Following her move from ESPN, Peters worked for TBN. There, she worked as a co-host for JUICE TV, a lifestyle program. She later worked as a morning show anchor and reporter for KOBI, NBC5, KOTI, and FOX 26 all based in the south of Oregon. Peters made another move in mid-October of 2016. This time she worked as a traffic reporter at CBS4 News; a duty she still carries out as of this writing.
