Who is Angie Craig?

Angie Craig is an American journalist and politician who supports the Democratic party.

Angie Craig is currently serving as the U.S. representative from the 2nd congressional district of Minnesota.

She completed her internship at The Commercial Appeal and joined the same company as a full-time reporter. She also served as HR at St. Jude Medical (2005-2017).

But this time, she won and became the first openly lesbian mother to be elected in Minnesota’s 2nd district to Congress. She was supported by 52.6%.
