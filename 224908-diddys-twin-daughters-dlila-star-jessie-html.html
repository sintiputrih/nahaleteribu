Diddy's Twin Daughters D'Lila Star and Jessie James Pose in Matching Black Dresses in New Pictures

Publish date: 2024-02-26

The Combs twins D'Lila Star and Jessie James Combs, daughters of Sean "Diddy" Combs, posted a lovely Instagram photo a few days ago. Diddy is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record executive.

D'Lila Star and Jessie James posted on Instagram a cute picture of them sitting by the poolside. They both had on the same outfit and struck the same pose.

It was quite the mirror effect with everything just right. The photo's caption captured its essence: "You can still look cute at home."

Justin Combs, Christian Combs, Quincy Brown, Sean "Diddy" Combs, D'Lila Combs, Chance Combs and Jessie Combs at the Pre-Grammy Gala and Grammy Salute to Industry Icons Honoring Diddy in Beverly Hills, California on January 25, 2020 | Photo: Getty Images

It is great to see that the twins are so upbeat and positive, especially after the tragic blow their family dealt with. In November 2018, their mother, Kim Porter, passed away. She went to sleep the night before but never woke the next morning.

The coroner confirmed that she was discovered unresponsive in her home and pronounced dead at the scene. The cause was later determined to be pneumonia.

Sean "Diddy" Combs and Kim Porter during the 2006 BET Awards | Photo: Getty Images

The love and support the family received were staggering. Tributes came pouring in from celebrities and people from all walks of life.

The family was obviously in the public eye, and Diddy did everything he could to make sure that the children didn't find out about Kim's death from the news. Diddy sent people out to gather the children so that they wouldn't have to learn about their mother's death from social media.

Since her passing, Diddy says that he is a lot more involved with the family because that is something Kim would've wanted. Though the family is doing well now, this grief has and will continue to affect them.

Diddy and Kim met while she was working part-time at Uptown Records, and Diddy thought that she was the "one." When they began dating, Kim was a single mother and insisted that her son, Quincy Brown, was part of the deal. This deal is something that Diddy still honors to this day.

Years later, they welcomed their son, Christian, and after that, the twins, D'Lila Star and Jessie. The couple's relationship did not exactly go down a smooth road and it was an on and off union.

Kim and Diddy only dated from 1994 to 2007. Just weeks before the twins, D'Lila Star and Jessie were born, Diddy's daughter, Chance Combs was born. This caused some pain in their relationship.

The strong woman took it all in stride and celebrated their family as is. Her last Instagram posts celebrated the family as a whole, including Diddy's eldest son, Justin, from a previous relationship.

Sean Combs and his children arrive at the Los Angeles Premiere Of "Can't Stop Won't Stop" at Writers Guild of America, West in Los Angeles, California on June 21, 2017 | Photo: Getty Images

Kim's untimely passing has had the effect of bringing the family closer together. It was rare for the family to all be together at the same time, but that all changed when they all posed together for a photo at the "Can't Stop, Won't Stop: The Bad Boy Story" premiere in LA.
