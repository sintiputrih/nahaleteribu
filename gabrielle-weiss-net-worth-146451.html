About

YouTube family vlogger who is known for her The Weiss Life channel. She appears alongside her mother, father, and sisters in frequent vlog entries detailing their everyday family life. 

Before Fame

Her parents created The Weiss Life YouTube channel on July 11, 2014. She was first featured in the video "HOW TO PULL OUT A TOOTH WITH A DOG!" posted on July 15, 2011. 

Trivia

She has helped The Weiss Life YouTube channel amass more than 370,000 subscribers since its creation. One of the channel's most popular videos, "Sidewalk Super Girls Episode 1 - The Hula Hooper," has earned more than 2 million total views. 

Family Life

Her parents are Jeff and Melissa Weiss. She is the oldest of her siblings. 

Associated With

Her sisters are Gracelynn, Gretchen, Gemma and Gwyneth Weiss.
