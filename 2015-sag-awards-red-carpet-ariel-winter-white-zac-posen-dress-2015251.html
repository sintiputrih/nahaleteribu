2015 SAG Awards Red Carpet: Ariel Winter, 16, Is a Bombshell in Dress

Publish date: 2024-07-14

This little lady's got curves!

Ariel Winter may only be 16 years old, but she's got curves for days and she knows how to flaunt them, as evidenced by her body-conscious ensemble at the 2015 Screen Actors Guild Awards on Sunday, Jan. 25, in Los Angeles.

PHOTOS: Everyone at the 2015 SAGs

The Modern Family actress slipped her hourglass frame into a tight, plunging, white Zac Posen dress, complete with a small train. To accessorize the style, she added pumps, coral earrings, and a Swarovski-studded Salvatore Ferragamo clutch.

For beauty, Winter styled her (new!) dark chocolate dresses into classic, Old Hollywood curls, completing the bombshell effect. "I'm a huge fan of change," she told E!'s Ross Mathews on the red carpet about her new hair hue. "I love to change my hair all the time. I change my nail color. It makes me feel new every time!"

The actress who voices Disney's Sofia the First also dished about just being able to attend such a huge Hollywood bash. "I really feels lucky because I'm [almost] 17, and I'm wearing amazing designers, and I'm here at the SAG Awards," Winter, whose birthday is Jan. 28, said. "It's really awesome."

And that's not the only thing Winter thinks is awesome; she's also really excited about costar and fellow bombshell Sofia Vergara's engagement to Joe Manganiello! "I would just love for them to have babies," she gushed. "Their babies are going to be so beautiful it's ridiculous."
