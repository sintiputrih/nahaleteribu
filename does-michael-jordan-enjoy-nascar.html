Does Michael Jordan enjoy NASCAR?

Publish date: 2024-07-01

Michael Jordan, the basketball legend, has made a significant foray into the world of NASCAR, raising questions about his enjoyment and involvement in the sport.

Michael Jordan's NASCAR Venture

Team Ownership

  • Michael Jordan co-owns 23XI Racing, a NASCAR team, with Denny Hamlin, a current NASCAR driver.
  • The team made its debut in NASCAR last year and fields two cars driven by Bubba Wallace and Kurt Busch.
  • Jordan's involvement as a co-owner has garnered significant interest from sponsors and fans alike due to his iconic status in the sports world.

Driving Real Change

  • Beyond the allure of sponsors, Jordan's involvement in NASCAR represents a chance to drive real change outside of the sport, particularly in terms of inclusivity and diversity.
  • Bubba Wallace, the only full-time African American driver in any of NASCAR’s three national series, rose to prominence through his activism to highlight racial injustice, which eventually led to NASCAR banning the display of Confederate flags at its tracks.
  • This message of inclusivity forms the backbone of 23XI Racing's mission and values, aiming to make a difference and change the sport by providing opportunities to underrepresented individuals.

Social Responsibility Initiatives

  • 23XI Racing has partnered with various brands, including McDonald’s, DoorDash, Columbia, Root Insurance, Toyota, and Dr Pepper, with a focus on social responsibility initiatives.
  • Notably, Dr Pepper supports the 23XI Speed Institute Tuition Programme, which awards scholarships to students from diverse backgrounds to explore motorsports-related fields.

Impact of Jordan's Involvement

Marketing and Brand Affinity

  • Jordan's involvement in NASCAR has had a significant impact on the team's marketing and brand affinity, aiming to be known beyond NASCAR and appeal to a diverse and global fan base.
  • 23XI Racing's unique approach, combined with the influence of Michael Jordan, has led to a younger and more diverse social media following, reaching wider global markets.

Intersection of Motorsport and Pop Culture

  • The team has strategically positioned itself at the intersection of motorsport and pop culture, working with partners to introduce NASCAR to the African American community and engage in non-traditional marketing initiatives.

Future Outlook

Long-term Mission and Goals

  • While focused on delivering short-term success, 23XI Racing aims to bring about long-term change in NASCAR, a mission that appeals to brands and partners.
  • The team's long-term vision includes creating a significant impact on the sport and attracting a wider and new fanbase, while keeping its core product and audience in place.

Potential for Success

  • Despite being a newcomer, the team's partnerships and engagement have already started to make a difference in NASCAR.
  • Jordan's iconic status and the team's approach could potentially lead to increased minority interest in NASCAR, contributing to the organization's efforts to diversify its fan base.

Michael Jordan's involvement in NASCAR through 23XI Racing signifies more than just a business venture. It reflects a commitment to driving change, promoting inclusivity, and appealing to a diverse and global fan base. As the team continues to grow and make an impact in NASCAR, Jordan's influence and vision are poised to leave a lasting impression on the sport.
