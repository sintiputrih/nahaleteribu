Shotzi Blackheart Reacts To Taking Botched Bump On Ring Apron On WWE NXT

Publish date: 2024-01-18

Shotzi Blackheart battled Dakota Kai on NXT this week. During their match, Blackheart took a really rough looking bump on the apron as she smashed her head during an offensive move. It wasn’t supposed to turn out like that.

Blackheart scared some people when she took that bump on the top of her head. She was able to shake it off and continue. Blackheart has been through worse, but it wasn’t supposed to be that way which she proved via an old clip.

I swear I wasnt trying to land on my head tonight….

Thankfully, Shotzi Blackheart is okay. She provided a clip of how the move was supposed to look for reference. Hopefully, she’ll nail it next time. You can check out clips below of the botched move and how it was supposed to turn out in two separate tweets.
