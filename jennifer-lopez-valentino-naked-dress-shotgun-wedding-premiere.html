    Jennifer Lopez is a woman of many talents. From internationally renowned singing sensation to revered screen star, the 53-year-old is anything but idle.

    Adding yet another string to her bow, JLo single handedly revived 2022's hottest party wear trend, bringing the naked dress into the new year.

    Proving that the skin-baring style is here to stay, Jennifer turned heads on the red carpet at the Los Angeles premiere of her latest film.

    Prime Video's romantic action-comedy is about a couple who jet off with their families for the ultimate destination wedding and run into trouble when the entire party is taken hostage. Sounds like the perfect antidote to the current January gloom.

    The Shotgun Wedding star brought back the naked dress trend for 2023 

    JLo seemed in high spirits as she rocked the most unreal gown by Valentino. Her spectacular piece was adorned with an abundance of crystals, painstakingly sewn onto beige mesh fabric.

    The high-necked dress featured long sleeves and a show stopping floor-grazing train. Layered underneath was a neutral bodysuit and a lemon-yellow bow tied around her waist, providing a sense of depth.

    The AW22 runway hero boasts many celebrity converts including Kate Moss, Emily Ratajkowski and Kendall Jenner, and now JLo has done us all a public service by bringing the naked dress trend into 2023. Billowing baby dolls and flowy shapes at Prada and Fendi respectively provided the perfect low-key visible lingerie accompaniments, but Jennifer's ultra-glitzy version is a cut above, especially where high-octane pizzazz is concerned.

    The star exuded her typical trademark glam 

    She topped off her look with vertiginous platforms, a pale yellow clutch and a dazzling pair of chandelier earrings, giving her look a sense of colour palette cohesion. Jennifer kept her hair understated in a slicked back bun, created by Andrew Fitzsimons, allowing her stunning gown and glamorous cool smoky eye to take centre stage.

    The star of the 2022 picture has clearly had weddings on the brain of late – understandable considering she and actor Ben Affleck tied the knot last summer. Jennifer looked breath-taking in her three custom Ralph Lauren gowns, but tbh – we reckon any upcoming brides who are feeling daring should 100% give her naked dress look a whirl…
