No DQ Match and More Rumored for Next Weeks WWE Raw, Roman Reigns Hypes Tonights Raw Main Even

Publish date: 2024-01-30

WWE Polls Fans on Conor McGregor

WWE.com has published a new poll asking fans which WWE Hall of Famer Conor McGregor reminds them of, and the choices are Ric Flair or Steve Austin. As of this writing, 75% went with Stone Cold.

No DQ Match and More Rumored for Next Week’s WWE Raw

According to PWInsider.com, word going around backstage in WWE is that next week’s Raw will feature Seth Rollins and Dean Ambrose vs. The Miz, Bo Dallas and Curtis Axel. Finn Balor vs. Elias Samson in a No DQ match is also in the works for next Monday’s show.

Roman Reigns Hypes Tonight’s Raw Main Event
