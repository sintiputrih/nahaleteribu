NJPW STRONG Independence Day Night One Results (7/4): Jon Moxley And More

Publish date: 2024-02-14

On July 4, New Japan Pro-Wrestling kicked off its NJPW Strong Independence Day event with the first night of action from Korakuen Hall in Tokyo, Japan. The event featured stars like Jon Moxley, Eddie Kingston, and Willow Nightingale, and it aired on NJPW World.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Ryusuke Taguchi, YOH & Oskar Leube def. Kengo, Dragon Libre & Takahiro Katori
  • Bad Dude Tito def. The DKC
  • Team Filthy (JR Kratos,Royce Isaacs & Jorel Nelson) def. Ryohei Oiwa, Lance Archer & Alex Zayne
  • Tom Lawlor def. Kosei Fujita
  • Eddie Kingston & Rocky Romero def. BULLET CLUB (KENTA & Gedo)
  • Momo Kohgo & Willow Nightingale def. Donna Del Mondo (Giulia & Thekla)
  • BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Gabe Kidd & Alex Coughlin) def. Bishamon (YOSHI-HASHI & Hirooki Goto) (c) to win the titles
  • BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Drilla Moloney & Clark Connors) def. Catch 2/2 (TJP & Francesco Akira) (c) to win the titles.
  • El Desperado & Jun Kasai def. Homicide & Jon Moxley

WrestleZone will have coverage of the second night of the show once it airs on July 5.
