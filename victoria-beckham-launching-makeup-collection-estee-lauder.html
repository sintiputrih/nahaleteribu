Victoria Beckham Is Launching a Makeup Collection with Este Lauder

Publish date: 2024-01-16

Victoria Beckham wants to spice up your regular beauty routine. The star is bringing her refined, modern eye seen in her wildly successful fashion, handbag, and accessories line to the beauty world. Beckham has just announced that she's teaming up with beauty mega-brand, Estée Lauder, to launch a limited-edition makeup collection that will hit select stores, victoriabeckham.com, and esteelauder.com in September 2016.

“I am thrilled to be launching this limited edition makeup collection with Estée Lauder,” Beckham said in a statement. “The whole process has been both inspiring and eye opening—from going back and revisiting the Estée Lauder archives through to working on the product with the Estée team, this feels like the perfect makeup partnership for me and my brand. I had long admired Estée Lauder the woman, and the powerful brand that she created, so I am excited to offer both of our customers this makeup range and play a small part in her vision for women.”

“We are excited to join forces and bring Victoria’s take on beauty to her many fans around the world,” Jane Hertzmark Hudis, group president of The Estée Lauder Companies said about the new collaboration. “Victoria is an entrepreneur in the true spirit of our founder, Estée Lauder, with a real understanding of what women want, and a commitment to making women look and feel their most beautiful. Victoria has a passion for beauty and we are delighted to welcome her into our brand.”

This isn't the first time Beckham has partner with a beauty brand. Back in 2014, she collaborated with Nails Inc. to create two limited-edition nail polish shades. The products to be included in her new makeup collection are still under wraps, but we're willing to bet the colors will be just as posh as expected.
