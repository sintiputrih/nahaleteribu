Roberto Firmino Sister Roberta Marcella: Parents &amp; Family

Publish date: 2024-02-07

What could be more special than a sibling’s bond? Exploring one of such precious bonds, let’s dive into the life of Roberto Firmino’s sister, Roberta Marcella.

Growing up with a younger sister, Roberto had always had someone who would look up to him, restraining him from taking any wrong steps.

During childhood, while Roberto was into soccer, his sister, Roberta, was more interested in makeup.

Despite having different interests, the Firmino brother-sister duo certainly have each other’s back.

A professional soccer player, Roberto Firmino, plays as a forward or midfielder for the Saudi Professional League club Al Ahli. 

After his first-team debut with Figueirense in 2009, he played four-and-a-half seasons at TSG Hoffenheim.

Nicknamed “engine,” Roberto gained fame for his creativity, goal-scoring ability, and work ethic while playing for Liverpool. 

During the 2019-20 season, he helped the team win two titles- UEFA Super Cup and the Premier League.

Additionally, he became the first Brazilian player to score 50 premier league goals.

After a stellar career in Liverpool, the “cult hero” begins his professional career with Al Ahil, signing a contract until 2026. 

Internationally, he has been representing the Brazil national team since 2014. With his impressive skill, Roberto has helped the team win the 2018 FIFA World Cup and 2019 Copa America titles.

Roberto Firmino Sister

Born to his parents, Jose Roberto and Mariana Cicera, Roberto Firmino grew up alongside his sister, Roberta Marcella.

Despite their poor economic condition, his sister stood as a great support for him, encouraging his passion.

Unlike her athletic brother, Roberta is not much into sports, following a different career path. Roberto’s sister, Roberta (born: January 30), is a professional makeup artist and blogger.

Moreover, she offers training as well as bridal and formal makeup services. Her Facebook and Instagram profile often features her client’s makeup pictures and videos.

Regarding her personal life, she is now married to Douglas Lima. They dated for a while before getting engaged on November 6, 2022.

Following their engagement, they tied the knot on June 24, 2023, and shared some moments from their wedding, showcasing their love.

Moreover, Roberta seems to enjoy traveling to various places, mainly to attend her brother’s games.

Roberto and his sister remain as close as they were during childhood when then-young Roberto’s career in soccer was yet to bloom.

Who Is Roberto Firmino Wife?

Roberto Firmino’s wife, Larissa Pereira (born: December 12, 1992), is a Brazilian model, fashionista, and influencer.

The couple initially met one another in 2013 in a nightclub, although they began dating only a year later.

After dating for a few years, Roberto and Larissa tied the knot in June 2017 in Maceio, Brazil.

While she has earned fame in the fashion world, the stunning Brazilian is also a key factor in Firmino’s success on and off the pitch.

Despite her popularity, Larissa has maintained her family details and background private, with no information accessible.

In addition, the two share four kids- Valentina Firmino, Bella Firmino, Liz Firmino, and Sophia.

Their daughter Valentina and Bella have a joint Instagram page under the username @tinabellafirmino with over 16.5k followers.

Along with their children, they share gorgeous family pictures on their respective handles, showing off their love.

Moreover, Larissa shared a few family pictures on April 7, 2023, after the birth of their fourth baby.
