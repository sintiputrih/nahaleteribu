Morena Baccarin children: Frances Laiz Setta Schenkkan, Julius Chick, Arthur

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Morena Silva de Vaz Setta Baccarin is a Brazilian actress known for portraying Inara Serra in the sci-fi series Firefly. Aside from her career, the actress is the of three lovely children. Read on to find out more about her children.

Morena Baccarin children: Frances Laiz Setta Schenkkan, Julius Chick, Arthur

Morena Baccarin has been married twice and has welcomed three children from those marriages.

In November 2011, she and American film producer and director Austin Chick, tied the knot. The couple welcomed a son, Julius Chick, in October 2013, two years after their wedding. Two years after the birth of their son, Chick filed for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences.

In September of the same year, Baccarin announced her plan to marry her Gotham co-star Ben McKenzie, adding that she was pregnant with their child.

On March 18, 2016, Baccarin’s and Chick’s divorce became official. In the same month, Baccarin and McKenzie welcomed their daughter.

In November 2016, they announced their engagement. They got married in Brooklyn, New York, on June 2, 2017, the same day as Baccarin’s 38th birthday. In March 2021, the couple announced the birth of their second child and first son.
