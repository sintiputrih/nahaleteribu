Kate Hudson Twins With Her Baby Daughter Rani Rose on Instagram

Publish date: 2024-07-10
  • In a photo posted to Instagram yesterday, Kate Hudson twinned with her adorable 10-month-old daughter, Rani Rose.
  • The actress’s famous friends, including Courteney Cox and Lena Headey, commented their approval with heart eyes and heart emojis.

For your daily dose of baby fever, head over to Kate Hudson’s Instagram. In a photo posted by the How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days actress, Hudson could be seen carrying her baby daughter, 10-month-old baby Rani Rose, while they both laughed and posed for the camera.

The mother-daughter duo were also adorably wearing matching outfits, each donning a white dress with ruffled sleeves and floral patterns. While Hudson paired her look with dark shades, a white handbag, bold beaded earrings, and layers of gold necklaces, Rani Rose simply held a stuffed rabbit in her hands for maximum cuteness. Hudson’s baby girl is growing up so fast!

Hudson simply captioned the photo with a sunflower emoji which is uber appropriate considering Rani's gorgeous smile.

The actress’s friends, including Diane Kruger (“She’s just too cute”) and Friends star Courteney Cox (“Cuties”), left heartwarming comments. Even Game of Thrones star Lena Heady, who played the cunningly evil Cersei Lannister on the Emmy Award-winning show, wrote, “Oh come on with the everything delicious,” accompanied with many heart eye emojis.

This isn’t the first sweet moment that Kate Hudson has shared on Instagram. On her 40th birthday, Hudson uploaded a snap taken with daughter Rani Rose, and her sons, Ryder and Bingham.
