Rhea Ripley Shows Off New Judgment Day-Themed Tattoo

Publish date: 2024-02-13

Rhea Ripley has worked very hard to become one of the top female Superstars in WWE. She also loves expressing herself via tattoos and it appears Ripley got yet another one recently.

The WWE Women’s Champion has gone through numerous changes over the years when it comes to her overall look and character. Many fans feel that her current character is the best version of Rhea Ripley so far.

The Eradicator already has a ton of tattoos on her body, which she uses to express herself as each and every tattoo has a message. Ripley took to her Instagram story and showed off yet another tattoo she got.

Rhea Ripley had her tattoo done at Built 4 Speed tattoo, as Ripley got a Judgment Day-themed tattoo on the back of her neck, which are her wings that fans see during Ripley’s entrance.

Rhea Ripley previously admitted that the women’s division suffered while she was busy fighting men. Regardless, we’ll have to see how WWE will continue to book Ripley as we approach the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event on January 27th.
