BBC Highest-Paid Woman Claudia Winkleman Opened The Romantic Aspects Of Her Husband Kris Thykier

Publish date: 2024-02-01

Claudia Winkleman is not a romantic person, as you think. As per express.co.uk, in an interview with You Magazine, Winkleman admitted that her husband, Kris Thykier is more romantic than her.

She mentioned, 'I'm not very romantic, so I usually forget.' Also, while featuring her husband in the interview, the Strictly Come Dancing presenter said, 'He's more romantic than me, but if we were both romantic, it would be disgusting, we'd just be licking each other's chins all the time.'

Undoubtedly, Thykier's romantic aspects have captived Winkleman's heart. Mirror.co.uk reports that in an interview with OK! Magazine, Winkleman said she completely fell 'head over heels' for Thykier due to his kind manner, flawless nature, and his incredible bed skills.

Winkleman said:

"I married him because he always asks the cab driver how his day was; I love the fact he’ll never take a seat on the tube; I’m happy he believes in big tips and not making a mess."

Claudia Winkleman and her husband, Kris Thykier (©: Express.co)

Winkleman and Thykier have been married for more than two decades now. The lovebirds tied a nuptial knot back on 24 June 2000 and have been together since then.

In the long excursion of their twenty years of married life, the couple has welcomed three kids, namely, Jake, Mathilda, and Arthur. The oldest, Jake, is 17-years-old, the middle child, Matilda, is 14, and their youngest one, Arthur, is nine.

Claudia Winkleman Net Worth And Salary

Through her career as a television presenter and a journalist, Claudia Winkleman has accumulated a net worth of $12 million. She was one of the highest-paid women on the BBC.

As per inews.co.uk, she earned an average salary between £370,000-£374,999. Moreover, Winkleman's earnings do not include the stint as the presenter of Strictly Come Dancing and her other TV and radio show for the BBC. So, she might have racked some hefty amount of fortunes.
