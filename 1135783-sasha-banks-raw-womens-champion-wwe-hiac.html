Its About (Boss) Time: Sasha Banks Needs A Title Win At Hell In A Cell

Publish date: 2024-06-10

As expected, WWE Clash of Champions 2019 was merely the beginning of the rivalry between Sasha Banks and Becky Lynch. The two would extend their rivalry and for the first time in three years, we will be getting a Women’s Hell in a Cell match. Who can forget the very first time involving Charlotte Flair and Sasha Banks? They went to war and The Boss will be going to war inside the Cell again for the Women’s Championship.

In all likeliness, Becky Lynch’s dream reign as RAW Women’s Champion may be coming to an end and there won’t be any real interferences in it. It may end up being one of the rare few clean losses that Becky Lynch has taken this year, but if the plan isn’t for Sasha Banks to win the title, it should be – and here are a few reasons why.
