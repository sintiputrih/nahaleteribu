Kubota M4030 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers, Attachments, Features & Pictures

Kubota M4030 Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota S2602 diesel 6-cylinder liquid-cooled 158.5 ci [2.6 L] engine, which delivers 48 hp [35.8 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

Kubota M4030 Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 2.6L 6-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, 4×4 MFWD 4WD (M4030DT), hydrostatic power steering, differential mechanical wet disc brakes, 3,968 lbs [1799 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 4078 to 7375 pounds weight, and 15.6 Qts [14.8 L] engine oil capacity.

Kubota M4030 Utility tractor attachments include a Kubota M1870A front-end loader.
