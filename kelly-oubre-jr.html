Kelly Oubre Jr.’s Professional Experience (Carrer and Highlights)

On June 25, 2015, the Atlanta Hawks selected Oubre with the 15th overall pick in the 2015 NBA Draft. Later, his draft rights were traded to the Washington Wizards in exchange for the rights to the 19th overall pick, Jerian Grant, and two second-round picks. He signed his rookie scale contract with the Wizards on July 9, 2015.

He averaged 16.8 points and 5.7 rebounds in six games while playing. In a loss to the Boston Celtics, he played exceptionally well. In 14 minutes of action, he had 7 points, 2 rebounds, and 2 assists. He also set a season-high in points in a loss to the San Antonio Spurs.

On December 17, 2018, Oubre was traded to the Phoenix Suns along with Austin Rivers in exchange for Trevor Ariza.
