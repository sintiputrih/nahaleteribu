BREAKDOWN THE BASICS

In the recruiting process for volleyball players, highlighting all of your measurables is crucial to catch the attention of college coaches. Your recruiting video should showcase your skills, athleticism, and be a strong first impression. Include drills that demonstrate speed, power, agility, ball control, setting footwork, and consistent reps. Focus on your best plays and keep the video within 3-5 minutes. Additionally, tracking and improving your hitting percentage can provide valuable insights into your volleyball game. Coaches often use hitting percentage as a measure of hitting efficiency, with a good percentage being 0.300 or higher. However, hitting percentage is not the only factor in player evaluation, and team dynamics and player development should also be considered.
