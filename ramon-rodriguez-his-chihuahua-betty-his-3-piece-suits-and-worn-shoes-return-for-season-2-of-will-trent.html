Will Trent Season 2 Release Date Revealed: What's in Store for Will and His Team?

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Will Trent Season 2 Release Date: Will Trent is a popular TV series based on books by Karin Slaughter. It tells the story of a man named Will Trent who works for the Georgia Bureau of Investigations (GBI). Will had a tough time growing up in Atlanta’s foster care system, and he struggles with dyslexia, which makes reading and writing difficult for him. But despite these challenges, he’s really good at solving difficult crimes.

When Is the Release Date for Season 2 of The Show Will Trent?

Will Trent Season 2 will be coming out on February 20, 2024. ABC decided to make another season of Will Trent on April 18, 2023, before the first season finished. This news came from Variety. ABC’s decision to renew the show before the end of the first season shows that they have a lot of faith in the adaptation of Karin Slaughter’s work. They believe it could become a long-lasting crime series.

Who Are the Actors in Season 2 of The Show Starring Will Trent?

In the next season of Will Trent, the key actors from the first season are coming back. These actors are:

  • Ramón Rodríguez will still play the role of Will Trent.
  • Erika Christensen will continue as Angie Polaski.
  • Iantha Richardson will return as Faith Mitchell.
  • Jake McLaughlin will still be Mike Furey.
  • Sonja Sohn will reprise her role as Amanda Wagner.

    Previous Season Recap

    In Season 1 of the show “Will Trent,” we meet Will Trent, who works as a special agent at the GBI (Georgia Bureau of Investigation). Will is really smart, but his life has been tough. He didn’t have an easy childhood because he grew up in foster care, which means he lived with different families instead of his own.

    Because of his rough past, Will especially sees the world. He understands things differently, and this helps him solve tough cases with the GBI. Even though he faced many challenges growing up, Will uses his experiences to help him in his job.

    During Season 1 of the show, Will and his team work on solving different crimes. They tackle cases like catching a serial killer, rescuing someone who was kidnapped, and figuring out what happened in a mass shooting. These are really hard cases, but Will and his team are up to the challenge.

    But it’s not just about the cases for Will. He also has his struggles to deal with. He has something called PTSD, which can make life difficult because he relives scary moments from his past. Plus, he doesn’t have a good relationship with his dad, which adds to his problems.

    So, Season 1 of “Will Trent” isn’t just about solving crimes. It’s also about Will facing his demons while trying to make the world a better place.
