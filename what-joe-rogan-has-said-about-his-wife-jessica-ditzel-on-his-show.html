Joe Rogan Seldom Shares Details About His Wife Jessica Ditzel, But Here's The Tea He Has Spilled On

Publish date: 2024-01-18

There may be tons of podcasters out there, but it's safe to say that Joe Rogan is the most popular. He's a wildly prolific podcaster who releases several episodes of The Joe Rogan Experience each week. And whether people love him or wish that he would stop making such controversial comments, he gets a lot of attention on a regular basis.

Rogan may share his viewpoints on almost every topic under the sun. But he doesn't often talk about who he's married to. Joe Rogan's wife Jessica Ditzel is super private, and people would love to know more about her. However, Joe Rogan has revealed a few things about what he likes about Jessica Ditzel and what their relationship is like.

10 Joe Rogan Said He, His Wife, And Daughters Prioritize Talking

While celebrities often appear on Joe Rogan's podcast and share their feelings and thoughts about various topics, he doesn't often get into his personal life. There are a few moments when he does, though, like in episode 1277 when he interviewed Gabrielle Reece.

Joe Rogan talked about his wife and his three daughters Lola, Rosy, and Kayja Rose. He said "I just give up most of the time. I lose every argument." Rogan also mentioned that he and his family discuss everything. He continued, "We communicate a lot. A lot of talking."

9 Joe Rogan Would Live On A Ranch If He And Jessica Ditzel Divorced

Joe Rogan married Jessica Ditzel in 2009, and from the rare comments that he made about her, they seem like they are happy. But he did talk about what his life would look like if they were ever to break up.

Rogan interviewed comedian Bert Kreischer in episode 1954 and said, “Bro if my wife ever leaves, I’m buying a ranch and inviting everybody to live there … I’ll buy a giant chunk of land and I’ll build everybody a house.” It doesn't appear likely that this is going to happen, but it sounds like he has given the subject some thought.

8 Joe Rogan Likes That His Wife Jessica Ditzel Is "Strong"

In the same interview with Kreischer, Joe Rogan shared why he loves Jessica Ditzel and paid her many compliments. He talked about how much he loves her personality and said, “I like a gal who could help you move a couch. That’s what I like. I like ’em sturdy..."

Rogan continued that while Ditzel didn't want her to say that she is "sturdy." He said, "My wife does not like when I say that about her. Just like when I call her sturdy. But she’s strong… I like strong women.”

7 Joe Rogan Let His Wife Jessica Ditzel And Daughters Decorate Their House

In his interview on The Joe Rogan Experience with Gabrielle Reece, Rogan shared that he didn't have much to do with the decor in his house. And he explained that is fine with him. It sounds like he wanted his wife and kids to feel free to make their house look the way that they wanted it to.

Rogan said during the podcast interview, "In terms of like how I decorate the house, I have no say." He joked about how the only things in a house that matter to him are having coffee, a TV, a comfortable bed, and a nice view.

6 Joe Rogan Likes That Jessica Ditzel Is "Dedicated" And "Kind"

In episode 1314 of The Joe Rogan Experience where he interviewed Andrew Schultz and Charlamagne tha God, Joe Rogan talked about what he thinks people should look for in a life partner. He said, "Find someone who you can grow with. You don't want to be with someone who doesn't share your values... You want to be with someone who's genuinely kind."

Rogan was honest about his wife Ditzel and he said, "She's a dedicated, disciplined person. She's also really nice." He added that "she's always smiling," which is something that he appreciates about her.

5 Joe Rogan Thinks His Wife Jessica Ditzel Is Funny

In another episode of The Joe Rogan Experience, Rogan shared that he thinks that Ditzel has a really great sense of humor. He explained that since he works as a comedian, he needed to find a partner who was hilarious. And it sounds like he loves Ditzel's jokes.

Rogan said of his wife, "I get along great with her." He added "she's easily as funny as me" and "she's got great timing." Rogan also talked about how when he and Ditzel go on double dates, she's hilarious.

4 Joe Rogan Doesn't Want To Know About Jessica Ditzel's Relationship History

When interviewing Bert Kreischer in episode number 1954, Joe Rogan opened up about his marriage. Kreischer asked him, “This is an intimate question, I have to ask this. When you met your wife, did you ever ask how many people you have been with?”

Rogan answered that he doesn't think about it. It's unclear if he has ever asked his wife about how many people she dated before they began their relationship. But either way, he says he doesn't focus on it.

3 Joe Rogan Needs To Write Jokes Without His Wife Jessica Ditzel There

Joe Rogan talked about his wife in episode 1863 and said that he likes to work on his comedic material by himself. According to Essentially Sports, Rogan said, “I physically run away from my wife when I have a joke idea."

It sounds like Rogan was kidding about that, as it's a pretty intense thing to say. But he was serious about how when he's in the zone, he wants to keep writing jokes without talking to anyone. It appears that Rogan and Ditzel have a great marriage and he has explained this to her.

2 Joe Rogan Talked About How Tough Marriage And Raising Kids Can Be

Joe Rogan gave Ben O'Brien tips about marriage in episode 1215. He spoke about the hard times that couples face when they become parents and their lives become busier and more overwhelming.

Rogan said, "a lot of marriages fail because of this intense pressure of the relationship." He said that he understands that people are interested in the legality of marriage when they are raising kids because it can be a stressful situation.

1 Joe Rogan Talked About How Both People In A Marriage Need To Put In The Effort

In his interview with Ben O'Brien, Rogan spoke more about marriage and said "You have to both have the mindset that you enjoy each other's company and you want to make it work. Some people just don't."

O'Brien agreed that a relationship is a "50/50" effort, and Rogan agreed with that. While Joe Rogan may not say much about his wife Jessica Ditzel on The Joe Rogan Experience, the tidbits that he has shared suggest that they're happy together.
