John Michael Higgins Biography

John Michael Higgins is an American actor and comedian who was born in Boston, Massachusetts, U.S. His film credits include Christopher Guest’s mockumentaries, the role of David Letterman in HBO’s The Late Shift as well as a starring role in the American version of Kath & Kim.

He played Peter Lovett in the TV Land original sitcom Happily Divorced and provided the voice of Iknik Blackstone Varrick in The Legend of Korra and Mini-Max in Big Hero 6: The Series. Also, he starred in the NBC sitcom Great News as Chuck Pierce for 2 seasons. Currently, he hosts the game show America Says, which earned him a year 2019 Daytime Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Game Show Host.
