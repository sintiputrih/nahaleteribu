Publish date: 0001-01-01

Kollam Sudhi Age, Height, Weight: Kollam Sudhi was a well-known Indian actor, comedian, and television personality.

He developed a love for acting at a younger age and was consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actors.

Sudhi started his career as a mimicry artist and soon became famous for his imitations of various celebrities

He became a household name following his role in the popular reality television show ‘Star Magic’ and was also a regular on other comedy shows, including Comedy Stars and Unplugged.

Sudhi starred in a number of films including; Kathari, Kattappanayile Hrithik Roshan, Theta Rappai, An International Local Story, Escape, and Keshu Ee Veedinte Nadhan, among others.

Sadly, the well-known actor and mimicry artist has died. Kollam Sudhi passed away in a road accident on Monday, June 5, 2023.

According to reports, the ‘Star Magic’ actor was traveling along with his friend, Ullas Aroor, Binu Adimali, and Mahesh when their car crashed into a goods carrier at Kaipamangalam, Thrissur (Kerala).

Kollam Sudhi sustained a fatal head injury and was rushed to the hospital but could not be saved. However, the other three survived and are currently under treatment.

When the news broke, many of his colleagues and industry players from the Malayalam film industry took to social media to mourn Sudhi’s demise.

‘Kadhal Desam’ actor Vineeth shared a picture of the actor and left some hearty condolences on his Instagram handle.

He wrote; “Woke up this morning hearing this terrible news of the tragic and untimely demise of Sudhi. Life is so unpredictable.

He was such a talented artist and making his mark in films. Recently I had the pleasure of working with Sudhi in an upcoming Malayalam film.

We shared many scenes together and he was such an extempore actor with a good sense of humour timing.

My heartfelt condolences to his family and prayers for the departed soul

Jeethu Joseph, director of the Mohanlal starring blockbuster ‘Drishyam’ wrote that he was feeling “shocked” at the unexpected demise.

He wrote; “Shocked to hear his unexpected demise. May his soul rest in peace.

At the time of this report, the family was planning his final funeral arrangement and the details are yet to be finalized. We will keep you updated.
