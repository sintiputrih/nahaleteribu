Tessa Brooks is an American dancer, model, YouTuber, and social media personality. Tessa Brooks has worked with The Pulse on Tour and has been a member of the ‘ImmaBEAST’ dance crew.

Early Life and Childhood

Tessa Brooks came to this beautiful world on 5th April 1999. She is originally from Fresno, California, USA. She is 24 years old and her birth sign is Aries. Moreover, she has two sisters, one of whom danced as part of Mommy and Me, dance classes. She also has a step-sister from her father.

Her mother’s name is Laurie Papagni. Other than this, she has not shared other information regarding her family members. Likewise, there are also no details regarding her educational background. She comes from a family of dancers and hopes to continue her legacy. Since there was always dancing going on in the family, she started her dance training at an early age and never looked back.

Apart from dancing and print modeling, she also loves to swim, act, play with her dogs, and spend time with her friends. Moreover, she loves keeping giant teddy bears in her room. She declares that ‘Cole Spouse’ is her celebrity crush because he seems artistic to her.

Career and Professional Life

She was exposed to dance and began training at the young age of two. Tessa realized that dancing allowed her to express herself freely. She began training in various dance styles and went on performing at various shows and gigs until she was spotted by a producer and was signed on shows like “Disney’s Next Big Thing”. Moreover, she is also a skillful gymnast and has performed as a cheerleader too.

Throughout her upgrading career, she was finally featured as a dancer in the YouTube movie ‘Dance Camp’. She continued making appearances for artists like T-Pain and got featured in X-Factor’s ‘Diamond White’ and Disney’s ‘Miss Coco Jones’. After performing in her share of events and concerts, she got an opportunity to be featured as a dancer on ‘The Pulse Tour’ and performed in some big events such as “Miss California Pageant”, “Disney’s Next Big Thing” and the “the Haiti Benefit Concert”.

Additionally, her talent made her a part of dance groups like ‘imaBEAST’ and ‘Team10’ crew where she got to perform at the Michael Jackson Tribute show at ‘Carnation Theatre’. Also as an actress, she has appeared in multiple national commercials. She also worked with Jake Paul, Erika Costell, and Chance Sutton during her time in Team 10. Though she is a successful dancer and a motivational public figure, she says that her number one goal in life will always be to inspire and help people, and to make a difference.
