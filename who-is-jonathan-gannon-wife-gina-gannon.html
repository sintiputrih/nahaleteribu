Who is Jonathan Gannon wife, Gina Gannon?

Publish date: 2024-02-11

Jonathan Gannon is an American football coach who is the head coach of the Arizona Cardinals of the National Football League (NFL).

Jonathan Gannon biography

Gannon, a native of Cleveland, Ohio, attended Saint Ignatius High School and excelled in three sports, winning a state title as a point guard in basketball, a district title as a hurdler in track, as well as being an outstanding wide receiver and defensive back.

Gannon continued to play until he sustained an injury that ended his career at the University of Louisville. Throughout the 2003–2005 seasons, he remained with the program as a volunteer assistant.

The following year, for Louisville’s victory in the 2006 Orange Bowl, he served as a graduate assistant.

Who is Jonathan Gannon wife?

He is married to Gina Gannon with whom he has three children.
