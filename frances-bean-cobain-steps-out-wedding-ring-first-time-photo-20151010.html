Frances Bean Cobain Steps Out With Wedding Ring for First Time

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Frances Bean Cobain was spotted out wearing her new wedding ring for the first time on Thursday, Oct. 8, since her secret wedding to longtime boyfriend Isaiah Silva last month.

The daughter of Courtney Love and the late Kurt Cobain, 23, stepped out in Cyprus, Calif., and the silver band on her left hand was clear to see.

The visual artist, who sported dyed black hair, wore dark shades, black fishnets, black shorts, and a white and red T-shirt that read “I’m a Toys ‘R’ Us Kid.”

Frances Bean and The Eeries frontman married the last week of September in a small ceremony, which her mother did not attend. A photo later appeared on Tumblr of the newlyweds cuddling together. The mom and her only child recently reconciled amid on-again, off-again drama, with Love sharing a sweet message for her daughter’s 23rd birthday in August.
