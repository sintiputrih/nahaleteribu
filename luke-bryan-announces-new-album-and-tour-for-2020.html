Luke Bryan Announces New Album and Tour for 2020 Celeb Secrets Country

Publish date: 2024-05-09

Luke Bryan‘s new announcement has been the best news we’ve gotten all year.

After playing to over 1 million fans in 2019 and being named the “Most-Heard Artist of the Decade” by Country Aircheck, Luke Bryan is back with the announcement of a new album AND new tour. The arrival of his seventh studio album, BORN HERE, LIVE HERE, DIE HERE is set to release on April 24 followed by his  “Proud To Be Right Here Tour” kicking off in May.

Bryan announced first to his fans today in a Facebook Live stream video, and continued to express his excitement by sharing the following tweet:

Excited to announce my #ProudToBeRightHereTour kicks off this summer with @MorganWallen, @runawayjune & @CayleeAnnaMusic. Select dates on sale 1/31. Head to my Facebook to learn how to win premium seats! pic.twitter.com/bOUAfb5QvQ

— Luke Bryan (@LukeBryanOnline) January 16, 2020

Joining Bryan on the 32-date tour will be openers Morgan Wallen along with Caylee Hammack and Runaway June at select dates. Kicking off in Cincinnati, OH on May 28, the full list of cities and dates can be found below and at LukeBryan.com.

Bryan’s upcoming album follows his chart-topping album, What Makes You Country, in 2017 which was his third consecutive album to debut at No.1 on Billboard’s Top 200 Chart, and marked his fifth No.1 debut on Billboard’s Top Country Albums Chart.

Check out a full list of tour dates below and let us know if you’ll be buying tickets by either leaving a reaction at the bottom of the post or by sending us a tweet at @CS_Country.
