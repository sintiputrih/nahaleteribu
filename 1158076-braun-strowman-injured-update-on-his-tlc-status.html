Braun Strowman Injured, Update On His TLC Status

Publish date: 2024-04-07

There’s a good reason why you haven’t seen Braun Strowman on WWE television lately: he’s injured.

As reported by the Wrestling Observer and Post Wrestling, Strowman has missed a house show in Jacksonville and SmackDown last week due to a minor injury. It’s said to be either a hip or back injury, but not one that is all that serious. He’s expected to be put in a TLC match against Shinsuke Nakamura if he winds up being cleared, although nothing is set in stone yet. Originally, the angle for their match was supposed to be shot last week.
