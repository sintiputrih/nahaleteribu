Professional career:

Reeves made her acting debut in 1989 with a TV movie called Mothers, Daughters and Lovers. She then appeared in several films and TV shows throughout the 1990s, such as Child’s Play 3, The X-Files, Kicking and Screaming, Smoke Signals, The Suburbans, An Element of Truth, and Escape to Witch Mountain. She also performed on stage in theater productions such as High Tor, House Arrest, Women of Manhattan, Picnic, and Last Night at Ballyhoo.

Reeves gained more recognition and fame for her role as Melissa Gold, the wife of Ari Gold, on the HBO comedy-drama series Entourage from 2004 to 2011. She received critical acclaim and six award nominations for her performance as the loyal but assertive spouse of a Hollywood agent. She also reprised her role in the film adaptation of Entourage in 2015.

Reeves has continued to work in various genres and mediums, appearing in films such as Mr. & Mrs. Smith, American Dreamz, Vicious Circle, Fugly!, Cosmic Sin, and The World Without You. She has also starred or guest-starred in TV shows such as Grey’s Anatomy, NCIS, Castle, Hawaii Five-0, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, White Collar, Covert Affairs, Famous in Love, and Paradise City. She has also produced a TV movie called Murder at Hollow Creek which is currently in post-production.
