Olivia Isabel Rodrigo, popularly known as Oliva Rodrigo, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. As of 2022, Olivia Rodrigo’s net worth is $5 million. At age twelve, she made her first screen appearance in an “Old Navy” TV commercial in 2015. Later that year, she debuted in the film “An American Girl: Grace Stirs Up Success”. She slowly started getting recognized due to her roles in “Bizaardvark” (2016) and “New Girl” (2017).

In 2019, she came into the spotlight thanks to a leading role in the Disney+ original series “High School Musical: The Musical: The Series”. Her popularity skyrocketed with the release of her debut solo song, “drivers license”, which broke several records and went to the top of global charts within a few days of its release in January 2021.

In February 2021, she appeared on the popular television talk show “Saturday Night Live” as a musical guest.

After the chart-busting success of “drivers license”, Rodrigo came up with two subsequent singles, “deja vu” and “good 4 u,” in April and May of the same year before releasing her debut album “Sour” in May 2021 to critical and commercial acclaim.

Following her rising fame and popularity among the young people, Rodrigo also visited the White House on 14 July 2021, where she attended the day’s press briefing and urged the nation’s youth to get vaccinated as soon as possible. She also starred in her documentary “Driving Home 2 U,” which was released towards the end of the first quarter of 2022.

In April 2022, Olivia Rodrigo won three Grammy Awards for her song “drivers license” and album “Sour”. She is also a recipient of the Brits Award 2022 for her single “good 4 u” and several other awards for her recent works. In total, she is a recipient of 20 awards and 15 nominations.

Aged 19 years (as of July 2022), Olivia Rodrigo has lately become a famous pop sensation around the world and has gained much fame and success in the early years of her career. As of July 2022, she has over 27.9 million followers on Instagram and 1.8 million followers on Twitter.

Having a knack for singing since early childhood, Rodrigo believes that “there is nothing that is a truer window into human emotion than music.”

Career

Olivia Rodrigo started her career as an actress in a commercial for Old Navy. In the year 2015, she did the role of Grace Thomas in the video film ‘An American Girl: Grace Stirs Up Success’.

In 2016, she was cast for the role of Paige Olvera for 3 seasons in the Disney Channel series ‘Bizaardvark’.

In the year 2017, she worked in an episode of the series ‘New Girl’ as Terrine. In February 2019, she was cast for the role of Nini Slakar-Roberts for the series ‘High School Musical: The Musical: The Series’ by Disney+ which premiered the same year in November.

For this shows soundtrack and promotional she worked on 5 songs which were ‘I Think I Kinda, You Know’ with Joshua Bassett, ‘Wondering’ with Julia Lester, ‘All I Want’, ‘Out Of The Old’, and ‘Just For A Moment’ with Joshua Bassett. Also, her promotional single ‘All I Want’ also managed to bag the certifications RIAA Gold and BPI Silver.

In the year 2020, she signed with Geffen and Interscope Records. On 8 January 2021 her debut single ‘Drivers License’ was released co-written by Dan Nigro, the producer, which broke Spotify records twice for the most daily streams ever for a non-holiday song and had a huge number of views.
