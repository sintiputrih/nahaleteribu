Patrick Wilson | Career

Patrick Wilson began his acting career from the movie My Sister’s Wedding in 2003.

Later, he was featured in the role of Viscount Raoul de Chagny in the romantic drama film The Phantom of the Opera in 2004.

Afterward, Wilson went on to play numerous movies, including Little Children, Running with the Scissors, and Life in Fight, among others.

After years of hard work, he got his major breakthrough and gained international fame from the superhero film Watchmen in 2009.

Moreover, Patrick Wilson is also known for his character as Orm Marius/Ocean Master in the DC movie Aquaman.

Wilson is mostly famous for his part in horror movies, including Insidious, The Conjuring, Annabelle Comes Home,The Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It.
